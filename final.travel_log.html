Utah, Home of wonderful deserts, arches, and flats.

Attractions

Bonneville is a beautiful place to spend your time, specifically the salt flats. You can spend hours enjoying the desolate yet brilliant scenery in the watery reflections and the grandiose mountains behind them. However, going to Bonneville could be dangerous if you don't know what you're doing because of the extreme conditions and the water sometimes making the salt flats into a slip and slide that might damage your car if you aren't careful.

Activities to enjoy at the salt flats

  • Leisure Drives with proper safety precautions.
  • Salt Flat Races
  • Photography and enjoyment of the scenery
  • Learning opportunities about how the salt flats were formed

This place is one of the most awe inspiring places I have ever seen in my life. The pristine feel of it all makes me feel like I'm walking in a land of pure crystal that shines with the sky's own coloring but even more vibrant.
